ABOUT

About

Ziberation Studio 是由一群藝術家、設計師和工程師合作組成的工作室，目前成員住在台灣、日本、法國和美國。即使專業背景不盡相同，我們都對科技、藝術、溝通、科學和設計抱持著熱誠。身在世界的不同角落，我們透過遠端合作，達到一個限制少且效率高的工作流程。我們相信彼此沉浸在多元的文化環境是個完美個機會，能促使我們互相學習、挑戰自我、且替未來創造獨特的遠景。

Ziberation Studio is a collective of multidisciplinary artists, designers, and engineers. Our current members locate in Taiwan, Japan, France and the United States. Coming from a variety of métier, we share our passion for technology, art, communication, science, and design. Being on different sides of the world, we collaborate remotely from our home offices, allowing a less restricted, more efficient workflow. Immersing in diverse cultures and environments, we believe it to be a perfect opportunity to learn from each other, challenge ourselves, and create unique possibilities for the future.

以智慧，積極地使用科技
以深情，敏銳地創造藝術
以膽識，有效地交流溝通
以高度的求知慾研究科學
以周全的同理心思考設計

Use technology vigorously and intelligently. Make art with deep emotions and fierce creativity. Communicate effectively and express ourselves courageously. Study science with curiosity and real comprehension. Design carefully, thoughtfully, and with great compassion.
